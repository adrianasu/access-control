Ready2Go

Are you looking for an automated system to regulate the entrance into your facility?

This app controls the access to a determined location based on configurable factors such as authorization or other requirements.

How it works

All people seeking entry into the facility, must be added to the system by an 'admin' user.
When attempting to enter, people will provide their ID to verify the status of the specified requirements
Then they'll be granted entrance or not.

Demo

A work environment with employees and contractors (all referred as employees) was set up. All employees are required to have a valid set of safety trainings in order to enter their workplace, a mine.

This app allows you to store and display additional information. In this case, the system was configured to handle employees, trainings, employers, departments and users information.
